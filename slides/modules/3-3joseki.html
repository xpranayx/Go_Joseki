3-3 Joseki

sansan

The 3-3 point emphasizes territory and speed (settling the corner in one move) over influence. Its weakness is the lack of obvious local follow ups. The number of 3-3 point josekis is very limited, so they are easy to learn. It aims for a close and complicated (i.e. fighting) game without big territories, where the corner territory secured with the 3-3 point may prove decisive. It is particularly popular for white as komi is a bigger advantage when neither player has much territory.
