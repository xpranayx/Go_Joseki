4-4 Joseki

hoshi

The 4-4 point (aka hoshi or 'star point') emphasizes influence, speed, and flexibility. Compared with the 3-4 point or 3-3 point, the corner can still be invaded and the territory is not secure. In modern go, it is one of the most popular openings and preferred by strong AI programs like AlphaGo.
